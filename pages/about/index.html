About

While I have a great many opinions on a wide variety of things, this blog is (mostly) dedicated to my contributions to Free and Open Source Software.

I’m an Ubuntu and Debian Developer. Check out my Launchpad profile, Ubuntu Wiki page, my Debian QA page, and my GitHub account for more details on my specific contributions.

On the non-FOSS front, I have a MS in Urban Affairs and on occasion considered referring to myself as an urbanist. I thought better of it, realizing how pretentious that sounds.
